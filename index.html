Starting a fitness routine can be simple. Begin with basic exercises, gradually increasing intensity and duration as you build strength and endurance.

Eating a balanced meal with carbs and protein before workouts fuels your energy. Post-workout, consume protein and carbs to aid muscle recovery.

Aim for at least 150 minutes of moderate aerobic activity or 75 minutes of vigorous activity per week, plus strength training twice a week.

The best exercise for weight loss combines cardio (like running, cycling, or swimming) and strength training to boost metabolism and burn calories.

Set realistic goals, track your progress, vary your workouts, and find a workout buddy to stay motivated and make exercise enjoyable.

You can calculate your BMI by dividing your weight in kilograms by the square of your height in meters. Our online BMI calculator can help!
